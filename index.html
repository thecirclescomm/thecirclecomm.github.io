What is The Circle?

The circle is a curated invite-only community of go-getters who are either in early stage of their startup journey or plan to start in the future, and understand the fundamentals of getting into a startup

The Circle Meetups

The circle meetups are closed 15 member brainstorming sessions over dinner, that happen twice a month, on every alternate weekend.

Who should attend?

  1. Individuals who are in the early stages of their startup
  2. Individuals who plan to start in the near future
  3. Individuals who are inclined to work with startups before starting one

Invitation Process

It is a closed curated 15 member event Preliminary screening is done on the above parameters to ensure a fruitful networking and learning experience for all members

The Circle's Mission

We realised there isn't a platform for 'Entrepreneurial Talent' to discuss & brainstorm ideas, get each other's input & insights on their early business model/product, as well as get guidance from industry veterans and entrepreneurs.

Hence, we decided to build a community of early stage to be entrepreneurs to enable them to kickstart their journey. It could be finding co-founders, doing market research, developing a unique insight, understanding their industry deeply, getting a sound advisory board, raising seed capital or something specific to their domain.
